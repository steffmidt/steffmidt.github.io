Who is Allen Iverson?

This website is about the NBA Hall of famer Allen Iverson. Iverson, born on the 7th of June, 1975, was one of the smallest players in NBA history, yet he ironically enough had one of if not the biggest influence on the NBA. Iverson was a combo guard (point guard/shooting guard) and has been awarded with 11x NBA All-Star, NBA MVP, 2x NBA All-Star Game MVP and many other awards.
